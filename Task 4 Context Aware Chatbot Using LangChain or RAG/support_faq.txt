TechNova Solutions — Support FAQ

Q: How do I reset my TechNova account password?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot password." Enter your registered email
address and you will receive a reset link within a few minutes. The link expires
after 24 hours.

Q: Can I connect CodePilot Cloud to a private GitHub repository?
A: Yes. During setup, grant the CodePilot Cloud GitHub App access to your private
repositories. You can restrict access to specific repositories rather than your
entire organization.

Q: What happens if I exceed my plan's repository limit on the Starter tier?
A: You will receive a notification and be prompted to upgrade to the Team tier.
Existing repositories already connected will continue to work, but you won't be
able to add new ones until you upgrade.

Q: Does TechNova offer a free trial?
A: Yes, all new accounts get a 14-day free trial of the Team tier, no credit card
required.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: Team and Enterprise customers can email support@technova.example or use the
in-app chat widget. Enterprise customers also have access to a dedicated Slack
channel with their customer success manager.
