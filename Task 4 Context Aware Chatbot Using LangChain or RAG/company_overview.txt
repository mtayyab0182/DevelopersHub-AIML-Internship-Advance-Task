TechNova Solutions — Company Overview

TechNova Solutions is a software company founded in 2015, headquartered in Lahore,
Pakistan, with a satellite office in Berlin, Germany. The company specializes in
building AI-powered developer tools and cloud infrastructure products.

TechNova's flagship product is CodePilot Cloud, a cloud-based platform that helps
engineering teams automate code reviews, detect security vulnerabilities, and
generate documentation using large language models. CodePilot Cloud was launched
in 2021 and now serves over 3,000 companies worldwide.

The company employs around 220 people across engineering, sales, and customer
success. TechNova's CEO is Amina Raza, who co-founded the company after working
as a senior engineer at a large cloud provider. The CTO, Daniyal Farooq, leads a
team of 90 engineers split across three product lines: CodePilot Cloud, DataMesh
(a data pipeline orchestration tool), and SecureScan (an automated security
auditing tool for containerized applications).

TechNova raised a Series B funding round of $40 million in 2023, led by Horizon
Ventures, to expand its AI research team and grow into new markets in Southeast
Asia.
